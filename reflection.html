Final Reflection

International Trade Portfolio

Most Interesting Project

The most interesting project for me was the Trade Dependency During COVID-19 presentation. This project showed how countries depend on global supply chains and how disruptions during the pandemic affected trade, production, and consumers worldwide. It helped me understand that globalization creates opportunities but also creates vulnerabilities when countries become too dependent on foreign suppliers.

Most Challenging Project

The Gravity Model activity was the most challenging because it required collecting data, performing calculations, and interpreting the results. I learned how economists use GDP and distance to predict trade flows and how real-world trade can differ from theoretical models due to political, cultural, and economic factors.

Most Valuable Lesson

The most valuable lesson I learned is that international trade creates mutual benefits through specialization and comparative advantage. However, countries must carefully manage trade dependency and develop resilient supply chains to reduce risks during crises such as COVID-19.

Skills Developed

  • Economic analysis and interpretation
  • Understanding comparative advantage and specialization
  • Trade policy evaluation
  • Data analysis and spreadsheet skills
  • Presentation and communication skills
  • Systems thinking and problem solving

How This Course Changed My Thinking

Before taking this course, I viewed trade mainly as the exchange of goods between countries. Now I understand how trade influences economic growth, consumer welfare, government policy, and global relationships. The course helped me recognize how interconnected modern economies have become and why international trade remains essential for development and prosperity.

Conclusion

This portfolio demonstrates my learning journey throughout the International Trade course. Through readings, activities, presentations, and projects, I developed a deeper understanding of global trade systems, economic theories, and real-world trade challenges. The knowledge gained from this course will be valuable in my future studies and professional career.

Joy Ahmed | International Trade Portfolio | Dong-A University
